The Cottage Nigeen

The Cottage Nigeen

The Cottage Nigeen was built decades ago. We have 6 rooms, 4 Deluxe and 2 Premium open for our guests, each room has a different view. They are done up in the shades of Pink, Green, Beige. The walls are walnut and Deodar labelled. The cottage has a heritage architecture of Kashmir. We have preserved its vintage look with its covered balcony (dub) Khatam band (wooden ceiling). It is fully carpeted. There are walnut-chests, divans, teak wood pieces and Tibetan Choksi. The pieces of artefacts have been collected on our travels and also some are gifted to us by our children.
